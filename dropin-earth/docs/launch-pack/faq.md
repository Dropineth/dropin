# FAQ

## What is Dropin Earth?

Dropin Earth is a testnet climate action interface for Tree Lotto, project evidence,
Impact Certificates, challenges, and public reports.

## What is Tree Lotto?

Tree Lotto is a testnet Plant & Enter participation flow. It does not add mainnet payment
rails in this campaign package.

## Is this real money?

No. The Great Green Wall campaign package is testnet-only and does not request mainnet funds.

## Are Leaf Points tokens?

No. Leaf Points are non-transferable testnet points only. They are not $CANOPY.

## Is an Impact Certificate a carbon credit?

No. An Impact Certificate is not a certified carbon credit.

## Is an RWA Fragment guaranteed yield?

No. An RWA Fragment is not guaranteed yield.

## Does $CANOPY offset carbon tax?

No. $CANOPY does not offset carbon tax.

## What is TON testnet?

TON testnet is a non-mainnet environment for rehearsing transaction verification.

## How do I report a bug?

Use the feedback page or Mini App feedback CTA.

## How do I submit a red-team challenge?

Open the red-team guide and submit through the Challenge Board.
