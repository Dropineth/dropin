# Risk Disclosure

This campaign is testnet-only and is intended for product, proof, and operational testing.

## No Mainnet Funds

The campaign package does not request or execute mainnet transfers. Payment Intents are
used for mock, manual, or testnet verification flows.

## No Token Distribution

Leaf Points are non-transferable testnet points only. They are not $CANOPY and are not
automatically exchangeable for tokens.

## No Carbon Credit Claim

An Impact Certificate is not a certified carbon credit. It is a proof record connected to
reviewed impact evidence.

## No Yield Claim

An RWA Fragment is not guaranteed yield. Yield-bearing RWA mechanics are outside this
testnet package.

## No Carbon Tax Claim

$CANOPY does not offset carbon tax.

## Challenge Boundary

Any public claim, proof object, payment status, evidence item, certificate, or report can
be challenged before it becomes trusted operating history.
