# User Guide

## Goal

Use the Great Green Wall Testnet Campaign to rehearse the Dropin Earth flow without
mainnet funds.

## Steps

1. Open the campaign page.
2. Read the testnet-only safety notices.
3. Join the campaign.
4. Open the active Tree Lotto round.
5. Create a Payment Intent.
6. Use mock/manual/TON testnet instructions only.
7. Plant & Enter after the Payment Intent is confirmed.
8. Check your Leaf Points.
9. Share a Co-Plant invite.
10. Open the public report and proof status.

## What You Receive

Leaf Points are non-transferable testnet points only. They are not $CANOPY, not yield,
and not a carbon tax offset.

## What To Report

Report confusing wording, payment status mismatches, missing safety notices, broken pages,
duplicate claims, suspicious evidence, or anything that looks like a carbon credit or yield
claim.
