# Product

Dropin Earth V1 is intentionally narrow. It proves that Dropin can move from raffle participation to verified impact without making premature carbon-credit or tax-offset claims.

V1 scope:

- 3D Earth-style public entry.
- Region planting progress.
- 2050 carbon gap ranges.
- Species recommendations.
- Tree Lotto fundraising.
- Deterministic `$CANOPY` drop.
- Deterministic RWA Fragment drop.
- Tree Planting Fund allocation record.
- Project and milestone management.
- Evidence upload, hash, and review.
- Impact Certificate generation.
- Red Team Challenge Layer.

V1 does not ship formal carbon credit trading, global yield RWA, complex DAO, cross-chain bridge, production robot scheduling, or carbon tax services.
